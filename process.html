Application Process

The application process for the Student Scholarship Program is simple and straightforward. Follow the steps below to apply:

  1. Visit the application page and carefully read all instructions.
  2. Fill out the scholarship application form with correct personal and academic details.
  3. Submit the required information and verify all details before submission.
  4. Applications will be reviewed by the scholarship team.
  5. Selected candidates will be contacted after the evaluation process.

Please ensure that all information provided is accurate. Incomplete or incorrect applications may be rejected.

For applying to the scholarship program, click the Apply button on the website and complete the Google Form.
