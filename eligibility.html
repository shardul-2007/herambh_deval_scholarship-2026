Eligibility Criteria

Students who wish to apply for the Student Scholarship Program must meet the following eligibility requirements:

  • The applicant must be a currently enrolled student in a recognized school or college.
  • The student should demonstrate academic dedication and commitment to their studies.
  • Applicants should show genuine financial need for educational support.
  • Students from all academic streams are welcome to apply.
  • The applicant must provide accurate information in the application form.

Meeting the eligibility criteria does not guarantee selection. Applications will be carefully reviewed, and deserving candidates will be shortlisted based on merit and need.
